    • Warning! Do Not Buy the PrestaShop WorldPay Module!

  • October 15

    • It has recently come to our attention that Official PrestaShop RBS WorldPay module will only work for European accounts. In North America, you must use the Authorize.net module to integrate with all RBS accounts. 

    The good news is that the Authorize.net module is 100% free, and the RBS representatives are more than happy to provide you with everything you need to set it up.
